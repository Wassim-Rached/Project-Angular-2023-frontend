Chaima from Elalia/Bizerte and Wassim from Douz/Kebili, are two students at "The Institut Supérieur des Études Technologiques de Charguia" who share a passion for technology and the environment. Collaborating on a scholastic project, we teamed up to create a website for the EcoSave club.

Wassim took charge of the technical side, using Django Rest Framework to build the backend. Simultaneously, Chaima, with her creative touch, worked side by side with Wassim on the frontend, using Angular to shape the website's look and feel.

Our website allows users to explore EcoSave's activities, register for events, and even join the club by sending requests. Users can easily monitor their registered activities. To enhance administrative control, we added an admin dashboard, accessible only with admin credentials. Admins can manage website data, including activities, club join requests, and registrations.

Beyond being a scholastic endeavor, our project reflects a commitment to using technology for a positive impact in our community, promoting both environmental awareness and community engagement. Explore our EcoSave website—a collaborative scholastic project making a meaningful difference!
